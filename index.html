The Lore

There are many names for the world renowned grilled cheese sandwich: the toasted sanswich, the cheese toastie, a hot sandwich.
However, billions around the world have come to accept the term grilled-cheese to represent the delicious amalgamation of toasted bread and dairy products that we all know and love!

The process

Just like coding or trying to sing along to bohemian raphsody without screwing up any of the words, learning how to make the perfect grilled cheese is a that is eaasy to learn, yet difficult to master.

As such we have taken it among ourselves as to educate the masses with the correct technique necessary to master your grilled cheese craft!

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of any bread
  • a generous helping of butter
  • cheese (any is fine but if it's not chedder you're wrong for that >:|)

Utensils:
  • Frying Pan (optional)
  • Sandwich toster (optional)
  • Butter knife
  • Cheese knife (optional)
  • Grater (optional)
The process
  1. Using butter knife, apply your generous serving of butter to the OUTSIDE of each slice of bread.
  2. Heat your frying pan or sandwich toaster
  3. Slice or grate cheese into inch wide strips and lay these carefully along the inside length of one buttered slice of bread.
  4. Assemble inside-out cheeese sandwich.
  5. When frying pan reaches 100 degrees plus or the indicator light on your toaster is green, place the sandwhich on the surface and listen for a light frying sound. If none is hear remove from the heat and allow surfadce to heat up more.
  6. Fry each side of sandwich until a golden crust has formed along the toasted surface.
  7. Remove from heat, plate immidiately with garnish/condiments of your choosing and enjoy while hot!
